# Group Contract

## Team Purpose and Objective

- Gain experience working in a software development team
- Improve our skills as software engineers
- Aim for the highest grades possible

## Common values and Standards

- Meet weekly as a team
- Keep everyone on the same page
- Mutual trust when it comes to completing assigned work
- Open communication
- Helping group members when they need it
- Staying up to date on slack messages
- Not putting people down when they make mistakes
- All ideas are welcome and valuable
- Taking initiative

## Roles, Responsibilities, and Accountability structures

- **Team Leads:** Ensuring everyone stays on track, being the single source of truth for when to meet, delegate work
  - Assigned to Unnati and Laksh
- **Meeting Notetakers:** Take notes for each meeting
- **Everyone:** Staying caught up on Slack, completing what they are assigned, communicating when they encounter issues

- **Accountability structure:** Communicate as soon as possible when you aren't able to get something done; otherwise, everyone is expected to complete assigned work by the due date.

## Potential issues and Conflict-resolution strategies

- Finding times that work for everyone to meet
  - Resolution: Using whentomeet.com and communicating on Slack
- Not being able to contact someone outside of meetings
  - Resolution: Message them on their phone directly; everyone is expected to respond to messages within 12 hours (less if close to a due date)
- Overburdened from work (too much to do)
  - Resolution: Communicating, splitting up work more
- Skill issue; we don't know how to do something
  - Resolution: Going to members that do know how to do something, office hours, being transparent

## Addressing rules not being followed

- Recommunicate expectations
- Involving group TA
- Find alternatives for what they can do

## Communication Protocols

1. Slack, expected response time < 12 hours
2. Phone number, used for emergencies (team member not responding on Slack)
3. Weekly team meetings
